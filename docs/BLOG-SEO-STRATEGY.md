## üöÄ Advanced SEO Tactics

### 1. Topic Clusters
Create "pillar posts" met satellite posts:

**Pillar**: "Ultimate Online Dating Gids 2026" (3000+ woorden)
**Satellites**:
- Week 1: "Beste Dating Apps voor Serieuze Relaties"
- Week 2: "Dating Profiel Foto Tips"
- Week 3: "Eerste Date Gesprekstips"
- Week 4: "Red Flags Herkennen Online Dating"

All satellites link BACK to pillar ‚Üí Google loves this!

### 2. Update Old Posts
Every month, pick 2 old posts and:
- Add 200-300 new words
- Update statistics/dates
- Add new internal links
- Re-publish with new date

Google sees: "This site keeps content fresh!"

### 3. Steal Featured Snippets
Find competitors' featured snippets:
```
1. Google search: "online dating tips"
2. Find featured snippet
3. Analyze format (list, table, paragraph)
4. Create BETTER version
5. Google replaces theirs with yours in 2-4 weeks
```

### 4. Internal Linking Strategy
Every new blog post should:
- Link to 3-5 older posts
- Get linked FROM 2-3 older posts (update them)
- Link to 1 Kennisbank article

Creates web ‚Üí Google discovers all content faster.

### 5. Schema Markup (Already Done!)
Your blog posts now have:
- ‚úÖ Article schema
- ‚úÖ BreadcrumbList schema
- ‚úÖ Author schema
- ‚úÖ PublishedDate schema

This gives you advantage over 80% of competitors!

## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå Don't:
1. Write for SEO only (sounds robotic)
2. Forget internal links (missed authority boost)
3. Ignore mobile formatting (60% traffic is mobile)
4. Skip image optimization (slow pages = lower rankings)
5. Publish without SEO title/description
6. Forget to update sitemap (auto-done in Next.js ‚úÖ)
7. Never update old posts (Google loves fresh content)

### ‚úÖ Do:
1. Write for humans first, SEO second
2. Add personal stories/examples
3. Use short paragraphs (mobile-friendly)
4. Compress images to < 200KB
5. Always preview before publishing
6. Monitor Google Search Console weekly
7. Update 2 old posts per month
